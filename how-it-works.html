Safety Infrastructure

Precise Logic for a Safer Home Environment

SafeFlame integrates seamlessly into your gas appliances, monitoring every molecule of air to prevent accidents before they occur.

  1. Step 01

    Sensor Watches

    The SafeFlame module utilizes industrial-grade electrochemical sensors to continuously monitor ambient air quality near gas fixtures 24/7.

  2. Step 02

    Gas Detected

    If combustible gas levels exceed a threshold of 5% LEL, our edge-computing processor validates the signal in milliseconds to eliminate false positives.

  3. Step 03

    Alarm Sounds

    An 85dB internal siren activates immediately while the LED indicator pulses coral, providing both audible and visual warnings for occupants.

  4. Step 04

    Phone Notified

    Simultaneously, a push notification is sent to all registered devices via encrypted Wi-Fi, letting you monitor the situation from anywhere in the world.
